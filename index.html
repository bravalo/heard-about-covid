I Heard About Covid 19

By Bethel Ann Ravalo

I heard about Covid 19

and quickly I have learned, life was changing in order to protect.

Now it is important to stay at home and watch out for our health. Keep a safe distance when outside we go yet keep connected to family and friends.

It is important to wash your hands with soap and wet your hands with water. To rub between your fingers and thumbs. And form a bubbly lather.

Remember your favorite place. Think of your school. Hold dear to family and friends that you will see soon! For now we all stay at home Study very hard, learn to be patient, helpful and good and always wash our hands!

Then one day will come when we will go outside. See family and friends laughing, running and playing together, side by side.

Bethel Ann Ravalo
